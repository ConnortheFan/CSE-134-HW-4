Tags used in the creation of this webpage

From the initial creation of this website, I was tasked to use 78 different HTML tags to demonstrate my mastery. However, as more features are added and the website becomes more refined, some tags may not be in use anymore as they don't serve a practical purpose.

I also made 2 custom tags my-shiny and console-hello.

my-shiny

my-shiny makes text extra shiny by making the background black and itself yellow.

console-hello

console-hello throws a greeting into the console
